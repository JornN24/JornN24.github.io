Shine Project

Shine Project Top down shooter T-Rex game

I have chosen for this project because it is one of my best projects I made. I spent a lot of time in it, I am also very proud of my self that I made this. I had many obstacles but managed to fix them. I learned to not rush my projects and to make a good to do list Also, I learned to keep going and to not give up when you can't find the problem or bug.

I used unity as engine and used C# to code everything. I also used the terrain tool to form the landscape where the player can play on. The buildings and enemies are assets form the asset store. I also made a start screen with settings using another scene.
