Top Down Shooter

Shine Project Top down shooter T-Rex game

This is one of my projects I am the proudest of it looks fun and works well. I also spend a lot of time in it, its a shame that I never had the chance to finish it. That is because I needed to make this during the shine project. I learned that working on two projects at the same time is hard and that I needed to plan it better. The project itself went smooth.

I used Unity as the engine and used C# to code everything. I made the player shoot little squares that destroy on impact. The zombies go towards the player and when they collide it does about 5-10 damage to the player.
